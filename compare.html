FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How quickly are servers provisioned? +
VPS and cloud instances are provisioned within 60‚Äì90 seconds of order. Dedicated servers are provisioned within 2‚Äì4 hours for standard configurations. Custom hardware builds may take 24‚Äì48 hours depending on availability and configuration complexity.
What operating systems are supported? +
We support all major Linux distributions including Ubuntu 20.04/22.04/24.04, Debian 11/12, CentOS Stream 8/9, AlmaLinux, Rocky Linux, Fedora, and Arch Linux. Windows Server 2019/2022 is available on VPS and Dedicated plans (additional licensing fee applies). Custom ISO uploads are supported on all VPS and dedicated plans.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment? +
No long-term contracts are required. All plans are month-to-month unless you choose to pre-pay annually for a discount (typically 15‚Äì20% off). Enterprise agreements may include SLA commitments, but billing is still monthly. Cancel anytime from your control panel.
What kind of support do you offer? +
All plans include 24/7/365 support via live chat and ticket system. VPS Pro and above include phone support during business hours. Dedicated and Enterprise plans include priority escalation paths, a dedicated support queue, and response times of under 15 minutes for P1 incidents. Enterprise clients get a named Technical Account Manager.
How does billing work? What payment methods do you accept? +
Billing is handled securely through Gumroad. We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans over $500/yr. All prices are in USD. Invoices are auto-generated and emailed monthly. You can manage payment methods directly from your billing dashboard.
What does your 99.99% uptime SLA mean in practice? +
99.99% uptime means no more than ~52 minutes of unplanned downtime per year. If we miss this SLA, you receive service credits: 10% of monthly fee for 99.9%‚Äì99.99% availability, 25% for 99%‚Äì99.9%, and 50% for below 99%. Credits are automatically applied to your next invoice. Enterprise plans include 99.999% SLA with enhanced credit terms.
Can I migrate my existing servers to NexusHost? +
Yes! We offer free assisted migration for VPS Pro and above plans. Our team will help you migrate websites, databases, and applications with minimal downtime. We support migrations from all major providers including AWS, DigitalOcean, Vultr, Linode/Akamai, Hetzner, and traditional cPanel hosts. Contact our migration team to get started.
Are there any bandwidth overage charges? +
Shared hosting plans have truly unlimited bandwidth within fair-use policy. VPS and Dedicated plans have a monthly transfer allowance; overages are billed at $0.01/GB. Cloud plans are pay-as-you-go with no surprise bills ‚Äî you set spending caps via the dashboard. Enterprise plans typically include unmetered bandwidth on their dedicated ports.
